React-Native Mobile App for Replay FX Convention



This is a mobile app built for the ReplayFX Convention that will take place this summer at the Pittsburgh Convention Center on July 27-30, 2017. Last year the convention was attended by 10,000 people. This convention is about gaming and is the home of the Pinburgh Championships with prize money of $100,000. The convention center will be filled with pinball and arcade games on free play, as well as many other activities.


In addition to being a scheduling app, it lists the arcade and pinball games. It displays the entire schedule organized by date and time. It also displays the schedule filtered by the type of event, such as competition, music, movie, etc. Another feature of the app is that it allows users to select "favorites" among the events to add them to their own schedule of favorites. Right now I am adding a feature which will display alerts when favorited events are coming up.


The app is currently available in private Beta for Android and iPhone.

Ruby Console Version of Conway's Game of Life



This simple, console version of Conway's Game of Life was one of my first coding 'ah-ha' moments.
